About Me

I’m a UI/UX Designer with experience in mobile-first design, front-end development, quality assurance, and launching new products. Over the last couple years, I have demonstrated proficiency in React.js, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and Java.

After a decade in the world of graphic design - print packaging and consumer products design - I pivoted to front-end web development, focusing on user interface (UI) and experience (UX) design.

My time in UX has enabled me to become highly adaptable and well-organized, while also fostering a user/customer-focused ethos and skills in project management, documentation, and prototyping.

Empathy and honesty are foundational to my work, and factor heavily in my decision-making and design processes. I thrive best in collaborative, Agile teams and enjoys working with stakeholders to make the best digital experience for users.

Some of my other interests include cats, video games, and home-brewing. I also really enjoy food, craft beer, and chatting about fun places around Cleveland.
